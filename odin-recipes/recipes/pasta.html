Pasta Recipe

Description

Pasta is a simple and versatile Italian dish made from cooked noodles served with a variety of sauces such as tomato, cream, or olive oil-based sauces.

It is quick to prepare, highly customizable, and can be enjoyed as a light meal or a hearty dinner.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Tomato sauce or cream sauce
  • Garlic and seasoning (salt, pepper, herbs)

Steps to Make Pasta

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente, then drain.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil or butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add your chosen sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Mix the cooked pasta into the sauce until well coated.
  6. Serve hot with optional cheese or herbs on top.
