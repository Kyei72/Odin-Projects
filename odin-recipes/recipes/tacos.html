Taco Recipe

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish made with folded tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients such as meat, vegetables, and cheese.

They are flavorful, customizable, and often topped with salsa, lettuce, and sauces for extra taste.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Lettuce, tomatoes, and onions
  • Cheese and salsa

Steps to Make Tacos

  1. Cook the ground meat in a pan with seasoning until fully cooked.
  2. Warm the tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  3. Prepare and chop vegetables like lettuce, tomatoes, and onions.
  4. Fill each tortilla with meat, vegetables, and cheese.
  5. Add salsa or sauce on top.
  6. Serve immediately while fresh and warm.
