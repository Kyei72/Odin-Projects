Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, cheese, and herbs. It is baked until golden and bubbly, creating a warm, comforting meal.

The combination of savory sauce, tender pasta, and melted cheese makes it a favorite for family dinners and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef (or minced meat)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (such as mozzarella or ricotta)

Steps to Make Lasagna

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef with onions, garlic, and tomato sauce. Let it simmer.
  2. Cook the lasagna pasta sheets according to package instructions, then drain.
  3. Mix or prepare the cheese layer using ricotta, mozzarella, or béchamel sauce.
  4. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: start with sauce, then pasta, cheese, and repeat.
  5. Top with extra cheese and bake in a preheated oven until golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it cool slightly before serving.
