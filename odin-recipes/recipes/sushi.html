Sushi Recipe

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice combined with seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.

It is known for its fresh flavors, light texture, and beautiful presentation, often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fresh fish or vegetables (such as salmon, cucumber, or avocado)
  • Rice vinegar, sugar, and salt

Steps to Make Sushi

  1. Cook the sushi rice and season it with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  2. Let the rice cool to room temperature.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly over it.
  4. Add your choice of fish or vegetables in a line across the rice.
  5. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat, then slice into pieces.
  6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
